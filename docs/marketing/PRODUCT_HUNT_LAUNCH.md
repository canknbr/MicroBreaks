# Product Hunt Launch Preparation

Complete guide for launching MicroBreaks on Product Hunt.

---

## Pre-Launch Checklist (T-30 days)

### Account & Community
- [ ] Create/update Product Hunt account
- [ ] Complete profile with photo and bio
- [ ] Start engaging with the community daily
- [ ] Upvote and comment on relevant products
- [ ] Build following (follow relevant makers/hunters)

### Product Preparation
- [ ] Create product page draft
- [ ] Prepare all assets (see below)
- [ ] Test all download links
- [ ] Ensure app is live and stable
- [ ] Prepare special offer (if any)

### Team Coordination
- [ ] Identify who will be "maker"
- [ ] Find a hunter (optional but helpful)
- [ ] Prepare team for launch day engagement
- [ ] Schedule time zones for 24-hour coverage

## Product Hunt Listing

### Tagline (60 characters max)
```
Smart screen break reminders with guided exercises
```

**Alternatives:**
- "Take better breaks, feel better"
- "Protect your eyes with smart break reminders"
- "The 20-20-20 rule made automatic"

### Description (260 characters max)
```
MicroBreaks reminds you to take regular breaks from screens with 50+ guided exercises for your eyes, neck, back, and more. 100% free, works offline, and your data never leaves your device. Build healthier screen habits today.
```

### Maker Comment
```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm [Name], the creator of MicroBreaks.

Like many of you, I spend most of my day in front of screens. After developing eye strain and neck pain, I started researching the importance of taking regular breaks.

The 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds) is clinically proven to reduce digital eye strain. But honestly, I kept forgetting to do it.

So I built MicroBreaks.

**What makes it different:**

🎯 **Smart reminders** that fit your work schedule
🧘 **50+ guided exercises** for eyes, neck, back, wrists, and breathing
📊 **Progress tracking** with streaks and achievements
🔒 **Privacy first** - all data stays on your device
💰 **100% free** - no subscriptions, no ads

I built this as a tool I needed myself, and now I'm excited to share it with the PH community.

I'd love your feedback! What features would you like to see? Any questions?

Happy hunting! 🚀
```

## Launch Day Strategy

### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best time:** 12:01 AM PST (Product Hunt resets)
- **Avoid:** Weekends, holidays, major tech announcements

### Hour-by-Hour Plan

**12:00 AM PST - Launch**
- [ ] Verify product is live
- [ ] Post maker comment immediately
- [ ] Share on personal social media

**12:30 AM - 2:00 AM PST**
- [ ] Reply to first comments
- [ ] Share in relevant Slack/Discord communities
- [ ] Email inner circle supporters

**6:00 AM - 9:00 AM PST**
- [ ] Post on Twitter with PH link
- [ ] Share on LinkedIn
- [ ] Reply to all new comments
- [ ] Thank early supporters

**12:00 PM - 3:00 PM PST**
- [ ] Mid-day update on social media
- [ ] Continue engaging with comments
- [ ] Share interesting stats/feedback

**6:00 PM - 9:00 PM PST**
- [ ] Evening push on social media
- [ ] Thank top supporters
- [ ] Address any issues/questions

**11:00 PM PST - Wrap Up**
- [ ] Final thank you comment
- [ ] Share final ranking/stats
- [ ] Plan follow-up content
