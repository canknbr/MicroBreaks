## Channel-Specific Strategies

### App Store Optimization (ASO)

**Keywords Strategy:**
- Primary: break reminder, screen break, eye strain
- Secondary: posture, wellness, productivity, health
- Long-tail: 20-20-20 rule, computer break reminder

**Review Strategy:**
- Request reviews after positive interactions
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- Address negative reviews professionally
- Update app to fix issues mentioned in reviews

### Social Media

**Instagram:**
- Post: 1x daily (tips, exercises, quotes)
- Stories: 2-3x daily (behind the scenes, polls)
- Reels: 2x weekly (exercise demos, tips)
- Hashtags: #screenbreak #eyehealth #wellness #selfcare

**Twitter/X:**
- Tweets: 3-5x daily
- Threads: 1x weekly (health tips, app features)
- Engage: Reply to mentions, join conversations
- Topics: wellness, remote work, productivity

**LinkedIn:**
- Posts: 2-3x weekly
- Focus: Remote work wellness, productivity
- Engage: Comment on relevant posts

### Email Marketing

**Launch Sequence:**
1. Welcome email (immediate)
2. Download reminder (24h after signup)
3. First break tips (3 days after download)
4. Feature highlight (7 days)
5. Review request (14 days)

### Content Marketing

**Blog Topics:**
- "The 20-20-20 Rule: Complete Guide"
- "5 Signs You're Spending Too Much Time on Screens"
- "How MicroBreaks Helped Me Reduce Eye Strain"
- "Home Office Ergonomics: A Complete Guide"
- "Screen Time Statistics [Year]"

### Press/PR

**Target Publications:**
- Tech: TechCrunch, The Verge, Product Hunt
- Wellness: Healthline, Well+Good
- Productivity: Lifehacker, Fast Company
- App Reviews: AppAdvice, 148Apps

## Emergency Playbook

### Critical Bug Found
1. Acknowledge publicly if widespread
2. Communicate timeline for fix
3. Push hotfix ASAP
4. Apologize and thank users for patience

### Negative Press/Reviews
1. Respond professionally
2. Address valid concerns
3. Don't argue or get defensive
4. Take conversation private if needed

### Server Issues
1. Communicate on social media
2. Update status page
3. Provide estimated resolution time
4. Follow up when resolved
