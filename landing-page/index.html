Most desk workers do not need a generic wellness app. They need the right 2 to 5 minute reset for the point in the day where eyes burn, shoulders lock up, posture collapses, or focus starts falling apart.

Pick the symptom that shows up first That is usually the cleanest entry point for the first habit.
Match it to the work pattern A meeting-heavy day needs a different reset rhythm than a long deep-work block.
Start smaller than you think The right starter plan is often one believable reset window, not a giant behavior overhaul.

Private by default, practical by design

Recovery data is personal. MicroBreaks keeps the core experience usable without forcing account setup, while still leaving room for optional sync and anonymous diagnostics that help stabilize the product.

  • Core recovery history stored locally on your device
  • No email or password required to start
  • Optional sync when cross-device continuity matters
  • Anonymous analytics and crash diagnostics for app stability
  • GDPR and KVKK compliant
